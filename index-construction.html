What We Do

Through a unique combination of engineering, construction and design disciplines and expertise, Concor delivers world class infrastructure solutions to customers and stakeholders across a broad range of industry sectors.

Our People

Ave Construction employs over 3 000 employees, the majority of whom are based on project sites. We embrace holistic development and support for employees with the aim of being a first-choice employer within our sectors.
